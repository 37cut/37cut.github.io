Some configs

Mouse acceleration:
/usr/share/X11/xorg.conf.d

50-mouse-accel.conf
Section "InputClass"
 Identifier ""
 Driver "libinput"
 MatchIsPointer "yes"
 Option "AccelProfile" "flat"
 Option "AccelSpeed" "-0.48"
EndSection

You can get your mouse with xinput command.
Then paste it inside of the Identifier quotes.

What is AccelProfile?
For example, when you are playing osu,
adaptive: Mouse movement is very important,
which you should put focus on mouse.
flat: Cursor movement is very important, which you just need to focus on the screen(cursor).
